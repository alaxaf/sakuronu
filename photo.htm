MiniDisc page

Everything Minidisc!

My main (and only) setup is MZ-N910 Net-MD capable recorder (release year 2003).
To record I previously used SonicStage on a WYSE thin client running Window 7 Embedded, but the SSD died in it, so now I'm using a WinXP VM. Not a fan of the new programs and websites, as they don't size up to how beautiful SonicStage is. It seems many people don't like it, but personally I love the way it works. Perhaps the only downside is the copy protection, but even 3rd party programs can't download music without a RH-1, or risking the TOS or even player. And it looks like the LP2 quality is inferior to that of SONY, so I'll keep using it for my mixes.
